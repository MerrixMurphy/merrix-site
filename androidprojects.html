Until Covid, I never really found my way into the developing world, instead I focused my efforts on maintaining life after university with my wonderful partner. Once I finally had free time, I decided to explore an app named Grasshopper. From there I excelled and soon wanted to make my own apps. Discovering Android Studio and Kotlin, I started doing just that. Within a few short months, I had multiple apps including a custom app drawer to feature them all in.

These are the apps:

  1. DevHub

    A custom app drawer and notepad hybrid made to keep all of my android coding in one place.

    Check out the DevHub on my GitHub!

  2. Break: Out

    A puzzle game in development that challenges the user to think outside of the box in order find the combinations for each lock.

    Check out Break: Out on my GitHub!

  3. Flashjap

    A customer app drawer and notepad hybrid made to keep all of my android coding in one place.

    Check out FlashJap on my GitHub!

  4. Paina Cafe

    An app I made based off of a resturant's website.

    Check out the app for Paina Cafe on my GitHub!

  5. RandomGen

    An app that helps to generate a random number, a random letter, or a random item from a list the user creates.

    Check out the RandomGen app on my GitHub!

  6. Secret Project

    An app asset I made when creating an escape room for my roommates.

    Check out the Secret Project on my GitHub!
